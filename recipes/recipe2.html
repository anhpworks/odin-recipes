Uncle Roger's Green Curry

Ingredients
  • white peppercorn
  • coriander seed
  • cumin seed
  • green chili
  • shallots
  • garlic
  • galangal
  • kaffir lime leaf
  • coriander root
  • lemongrass
  • shrimp paste
  • fresh coconut milk
  • chicken thigh
  • fish sauce
  • thai eggplant
  • thai basil
  • red chili
  • bamboo shoot
Steps
  1. Toast your spices until fragrant(white peppercorn, coriander seed, cumin seed)
  2. Pound freshly toasted spice in pestle and mortar and pour it out when done
  3. Chop up your chili
  4. Put chili, shallot, garlic and galangal,kaffir lime leaf, lemongrass, and coriander root in mortal and pestle and pound
  5. As you pound add your ground spices to add friction and make it faster and some salt
  6. Add shrimp paste when done pounding and continue
  7. Add coconut milk to hot oil in wok
  8. Put in green curry paste
  9. Put in chicken thigh
  10. Add in fish sauce
  11. Put in thai eggplant, thai basil, red chili bamboo shoot and kaffir lime leaf
  12. Add palm sugar
  13. Add MSG
  14. Serve and eat while you judge jamie Oliver
