Uncle Roger's Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients: No measure. Stop when your ancestors tell you to.
  • Day old cold rice
  • Spring Onion
  • Scallots
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Chili
  • Soy Sauce
  • Asian Sesame Oil
  • Pork lard OR peanut oil (or vegetable if you weak)
  • white peppa (optional)
  • and of course... MSG
Steps
  1. Oil the wok with fat of choice
  2. Fry garlic and shallot until fragrant
  3. Add egg, fry until fluffy
  4. Add rice. Stir and break rice balls
  5. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, MSG, white peppa. Stir
  6. Add spring onion, chili
  7. TOSS!
  8. Enjoy your egg fried rice while judging Jamie Oliver
